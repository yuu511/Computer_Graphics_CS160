Select a cylinder and press "s" to shear or "t" to twist.

Press "r" to start / stop indefinite rotation , q for square movement. Note that buttons stop eachothers movement, and will only start after a stop.

Press "f" for falldown

Selection highlighting is turned off until the mouse is released. To move a cylinder, click and keep the button held down (clicking an already highlighted cylinder will de-highlight it)
